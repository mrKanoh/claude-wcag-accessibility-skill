# Prompt: Summarize audit findings for stakeholders

Use when you have raw audit findings and need an exec-friendly summary.

---

## Prompt (copy & paste)

```
Summarize the following accessibility audit findings into an executive summary for non-technical stakeholders.

Requirements:
- Plain language — no jargon (or explain any technical term in parentheses)
- Lead with business risk: legal exposure, user exclusion numbers, brand impact
- Group issues by severity (Critical / Serious / Moderate / Minor)
- Highlight the top 3 issues that need immediate attention
- Include a one-paragraph description of what each critical issue means for a real user
- End with a recommended 90-day remediation plan with milestone dates

Legal context: [e.g. "EAA applies to our EU products from 28 June 2025" / "Section 508 audit required for government contract"]
Audience: [e.g. "VP of Product and Legal team"]

Raw findings:
[PASTE findings table or list here — include SC, severity, and description]
```
